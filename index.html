THE BREWMASTER
Born and raised in Seattle, Cyrus Shahrivar loves himself some beer and leisure. After moving to Harlem, he started brewing small delicate batches of flavorful, aromatic beers that would perk the interests of even the most judgemental humans on earth - New Yorkers. Harnessing the vices and virtues of his peers and himself, Grand Penguin Beer Company was born.
